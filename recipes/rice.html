Chicken Fried Rice

Description

Chicken fried rice is a flavorful and versatile dish that combines tender chicken, fluffy rice, and a mix of vegetables, all stir-fried in a savory sauce. It's a quick and easy meal that's perfect for using up leftover rice and makes for a satisfying dinner or lunch.

This recipe is customizable, so feel free to add your favorite vegetables or adjust the seasoning to suit your taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked and cooled rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken (shredded or diced)
  • 1/2 cup carrots (finely diced)
  • 1/2 cup peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (diced, optional)
  • 2 eggs (lightly beaten)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Green onions (chopped, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the beaten eggs to the skillet and scramble them until fully cooked. Remove the eggs from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil. Saut√© the garlic and onions until fragrant and lightly golden.
  4. Add the diced carrots, peas, and bell peppers (if using) to the skillet. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Add the cooked chicken to the skillet and stir to combine with the vegetables.
  6. Add the cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the rice is heated through.
  7. Pour in the soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and sesame oil. Mix well to evenly coat the rice and vegetables.
  8. Add the scrambled eggs back to the skillet and stir to combine.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for another 1-2 minutes, stirring frequently.
  10. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious chicken fried rice!
